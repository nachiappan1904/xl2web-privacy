FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

Does XL2Web work on any website?

XL2Web works with most standard web forms. Highly dynamic pages may require wait steps or re-selecting fields if the site changes.

Is my spreadsheet uploaded anywhere?

Your spreadsheet is processed in your browser. XL2Web does not upload your file to a server by default.

Can it handle multi-page forms?

Yes. Configure multiple pages for a website and fill them step-by-step using manual fill or Auto-fill on navigation.

Do I need coding knowledge?

No. You select fields by clicking on the page and choose mappings from dropdowns.
